5. keys:

			[LEFT arrow] --- Change focus to left column
			[RIGHT arrow] --- Change focus to right column

			[DOWN arrow] --- Change focus to below row
			[F2] --- select all selection
			[F3] --- deselect all selection
			[F4] --- invert all selection
